My Favorite Pokemon

You Just Gotta Pick and Choose!

Here are couple of my favorite pokemon. (They are all water type!! =^._.^= )

Vaporeon

Vaporeon an aqua jet pokemon. It is known for being able to dissolve into water at it's will. This spokemon evolves from eevee.

Suicune

This pokemon is a legendary water type. This pokemon is part of the legendary beast trio from generation 2. The trio also includes Entei and Raikou. This pokemon does not evolve.

Kyogre

Kyorge is also a legendary pokemon. It often clashes with a pokemon named Groudon, a fire type.This pokemon also has a primal version. It does not evolve.

Gyarados

This pokemon evolves from th week pokemon magikarp. Did you know that male Gyarados have blue whiskers and female Gyarados have white whiskers?

What are Pokemon?

Welcome to the world of pokemon! Pokemon are creatures of all shapes and sizes. They are trained and raised by their owners, or trainers. Certain Pokemon can be found in certain regions. In the whole Pokemon world, there are over 700 Pokemon!

Types of Pokemon

There are 18 different types of Pokemon. They are water, steel, rock, normal, poison, pshycic,ice, ground, grass, ghost, flying, fire, fighting, fairy, electric, bug, dark, and dragon. Each pokemon has a different type, and some have two types. For example, Pikachu is electric type, while Snowver is grass and ice type.

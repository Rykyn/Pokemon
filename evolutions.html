What are Pokemon Evolutions?

Almost all pokemon have something called an evolution, which means that the pokemon can evolve into something different. For example, a pokemon like Bulbasuar evolves into Ivysuar. Ivysuar later evolves into Venasuar. Pokemon GO has this feature. When you catch enough pokemon, yuo will get enough candies. You will be able to evolve your pokemon.

Pidgey

Pidgey is the first evolution of the three pokemon. In pokemon go, you need a certain amount of candies to evolve it. Some pokemon need a special item to evolve. You can get these special items by many different ways.

Pidgeotto

Pidgeotto is the next evolution. Sometimes, the pokemon can change type. When you want to evolve it, it takes more candy.

Pidgeot

This pidgeot is the last evolution of pidgey. It evolves from the pidgeotto. This pokemon does not evolve anymore, so you can only power it up to make it stronger.
